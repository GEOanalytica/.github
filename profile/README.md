# GEOanalytica

GEOanalytica builds AutoRank, an AI visibility platform for teams that need to understand and improve how their brand appears in AI-powered search.

AutoRank helps marketers and agencies track brand mentions, source citations, sentiment, share of voice, and competitor visibility across platforms such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini. The platform turns that data into prioritized actions for content, technical improvements, and authority building.

What we work on:

- AI search visibility monitoring
- Generative Engine Optimization recommendations
- Competitor benchmarking
- Source and citation analytics
- Daily prompt tracking and reporting

Most product development happens in private repositories. This public profile is here to make the organization recognizable and point people to the right place.
